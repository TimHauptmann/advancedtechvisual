Dassault Aviation SA

Dassault Aviation SA is a French aerospace company that shapes the future by designing and building military aircraft, business jets and space systems. Dassault Aviation SA was founded in 1929 by Marcel Dassault, a pioneer in aviation and aeronautics. The company started as a manufacturer of propeller-driven aircraft, such as the MB.200 bomber and the MD.450 Ouragan fighter. The company later developed the Mirage series of jet fighters, which were widely used by various air forces around the world. The company also produced the Mystere-Falcon, the first business jet in Europe, and the Mercure, a short-range airliner.

The company is currently a subsidiary of Groupe Industriel Marcel Dassault SA, a holding company that also owns stakes in Thales, a global leader in aerospace, defense, security and transportation.
