Flexible Display

November 1, 2023

Flexible display technology works by using a flexible substrate, such as plastic, instead of traditional glass substrates. This allows the display to bend and flex without breaking, making it possible to create curved or foldable screens. The flexible substrate is coated with a layer of conductive material, such as indium tin oxide (ITO), which forms the basis for the display's electronic circuitry. The display itself can be made using a variety of technologies, including OLED, LCD, or e-paper. Overall, flexible display technology provides a new level of versatility in terms of form factor and opens up new possibilities for product design, especially for wearable devices and foldable smartphones.
