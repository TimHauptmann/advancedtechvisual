Tusaş Engine Industries

Tusaş Engine Industries is a prominent Turkish aerospace company that has been operating since 1985. The company specializes in the design, development, and production of aircraft engines and related components, and is headquartered in Ankara, Turkey. As a subsidiary of Turkish Aerospace Industries (TAI), Tusaş Engine Industries has established partnerships with several international companies in the aerospace industry, which has helped the company to expand its reach and expertise.

Tusaş Engine Industries offers a wide range of products, including turbofan, turboprop, and turboshaft engines, as well as engine components such as compressors, turbines, and combustion chambers. The company's engines are used in a variety of aircraft, including military and civilian planes, helicopters, and unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). Tusaş Engine Industries is committed to innovation and sustainability in its operations and products, and has invested heavily in research and development to stay at the forefront of the industry.

In addition to its core business of engine design and production, Tusaş Engine Industries also provides maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) services for its products. The company has a state-of-the-art facility in Ankara that is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by highly trained technicians. This allows Tusaş Engine Industries to provide its customers with comprehensive support throughout the lifecycle of their engines, from initial installation to ongoing maintenance and repair.

Overall, Tusaş Engine Industries is a leading player in the global aerospace industry, with a strong reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability. The company's commitment to sustainability and customer service has helped it to build long-term relationships with customers around the world, and to maintain its position as a trusted partner in the aerospace industry.
