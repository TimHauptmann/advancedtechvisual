Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation

Powerchip Semiconductor Manufacturing Corporation (PSMC) is a leading Taiwan-based company that specializes in the production and distribution of dynamic random-access memory (DRAM) products. Established in 1994, PSMC has since grown to become one of the most reputable manufacturers of DRAM products globally, boasting a vast product portfolio that includes DDR4, DDR3, DDR2, and DDR SDRAM.

PSMC's products are used in a diverse range of applications, including but not limited to, computers, mobile devices, servers, and other electronic devices. The company has a global presence with a network of offices and facilities in the Asia-Pacific region, Europe, and North America. As a testament to its commitment to providing customers with top-quality products and services, PSMC has received numerous awards and recognitions, such as the National Award of Outstanding SMEs in Taiwan, the Excellent Quality Achievement Award, and the Best Supplier Award from leading industry players.

Moreover, PSMC operates under a set of guiding principles that prioritize customer satisfaction, innovation, and corporate social responsibility. The company constantly invests in research and development to deliver cutting-edge technology and is dedicated to minimizing its environmental impact through sustainable manufacturing processes. PSMC's unwavering commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has helped it establish itself as a trusted and reliable partner to customers worldwide.
