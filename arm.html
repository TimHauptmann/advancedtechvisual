Arm Holdings plc

Arm Holdings is a globally recognized British semiconductor and software design company that specializes in creating and developing advanced and energy-efficient processors for a wide range of applications. Established in 1990, the company has its headquarters in Cambridge, England, and is a leader in designing processors for mobile devices, servers, and embedded systems.

Arm's innovative processors are widely used in various applications, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, set-top boxes, automotive systems, and many other electronic devices. The company licenses its processor designs to other manufacturers, who can then customize, produce and sell products based on those designs.

In addition to processor designs, Arm also develops software and tools to support developers in creating software for their processors. This includes operating systems, software development tools, and other resources that help developers optimize their software for Arm processors.

In 2016, SoftBank Group Corp acquired Arm Holdings, and the company continues to be a leading provider of cutting-edge technology solutions to businesses and individuals around the world.
