Pratt & Whitney

Pratt & Whitney is a leader in the engineering and manufacturing of aircraft engines and auxiliary power units. The company was founded in 1860 and is headquartered in East Hartford, Connecticut. It is a subsidiary of United Technologies Corporation, one of the world’s largest aerospace companies. Pratt & Whitney designs and manufactures a wide range of state-of-the-art engines for commercial, military, and business aircraft. Their engines are used by a variety of customers including airlines, the U.S. government, and private companies. The company’s product line includes the PW1000G PurePower® Geared Turbofan™ engine, the F135 engine for the F-35 Lightning II, the F119 engine for the F-22 Raptor, and the F100 engine for the F-16 Fighting Falcon. They also produce the PT6 family of turboprop engines and the JT15D turbofan engine.

Pratt & Whitney’s commitment to quality and innovation has earned them numerous awards and recognitions. Their success is also attributed to their focus on environmental stewardship. They are committed to reducing environmental impact and conserving resources. This includes reducing air emissions, energy consumption, and water use. In addition, they are constantly striving to develop more efficient and reliable engines.

Pratt & Whitney is a trusted and reliable partner for the aerospace industry. They are dedicated to delivering reliable and cost-effective solutions to their customers. Their commitment to excellence and innovation helps them stay ahead of their competition. They are committed to providing superior customer service, quality products, and advanced technology to their customers.
