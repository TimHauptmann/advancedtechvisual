Tower Semiconductor Ltd.

Tower Semiconductor Ltd., also known as TowerJazz, is a semiconductor foundry that operates globally, with its headquarters located in Israel. The company specializes in the manufacturing of integrated circuits (ICs) using advanced process technologies. TowerJazz offers a wide range of customizable process platforms that cater to a diverse array of industries, such as automotive, medical, industrial, and consumer electronics.

The company's portfolio comprises advanced CMOS, power management, mixed-signal, and radio frequency (RF) technologies. These technologies enable TowerJazz to provide its customers with a comprehensive suite of semiconductor solutions that support a broad range of applications.

TowerJazz's fabrication plants are located in Israel, the United States, and Japan, with a total manufacturing capacity of approximately 1 million wafers per year. The company is committed to innovation and customer satisfaction, and has a track record of providing its clients with high-quality products and services. With its state-of-the-art technologies and global presence, TowerJazz is well-positioned to continue its growth and success in the semiconductor industry.
