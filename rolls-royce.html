Rolls-Royce Holdings plc

Rolls-Royce Holdings plc is an engineering company incorporated in England and Wales that designs, manufactures and distributes power systems. Founded by Charles Rolls and Henry Royce in 1906, the company has since evolved to become a global leader in the aerospace, defence and energy markets. Its products include engines for aircraft, helicopters, ships and submarines, as well as power systems for the military and nuclear industries.

Rolls-Royce also provides aftermarket services, such as maintenance, repair and support for its products. In addition, it manufactures industrial gas turbines, marine and aero-derivative gas turbines, and nuclear power plants. The company also provides technologies for the oil and gas industry, and has actively engaged in the development of renewable energy sources. Rolls-Royce is committed to innovation and is constantly investing in research and development to ensure they remain at the cutting edge of the industry, with a focus on developing new technologies that are more efficient, reliable and environmentally friendly.

Rolls-Royce is a key player in the global market, and its products and services are used by customers in more than 120 countries. It has a strong presence in the UK, where it is headquartered, and in the US, Germany, France, India and China. The company has an extensive network of offices and factories around the world and employs over 50,000 people.
